Welcome!

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The University of Hong Kong. My advisor is Prof. Edmund Lam.

My current research interest is Computational Imaging and inverse problems, with more emphasis on Photography. I’ve investigated sparse CT reconstruction, light field holographic rendering, deflectometry for 3D surface profile. I’m also broadly interested in learning computer theory and algorithms analysis, such as randomized algorithms and optimization algorithms.

In my spare time, I love hiking, running, and all kinds of sports. Thriller is my favorate type of movies.
