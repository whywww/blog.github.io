Welcome!

I am a Master student studying Computer Science at Northwestern University, IL, advised by Prof. Oliver Cossairt. I received my B.Eng. degree in Software Engineering at Sichuan University in 2019, supervised by Prof. Jiancheng Lv. I’ve also attended the Summer workshop at School of Computing, National University of Singapore in Big Data & Cloud Computing with full scholarship.

My current research interest combines Computer Vision and Computational Photography, with more emphasis on medical imaging reconstruction problems. I’ve investigated light field holographic rendering, deflectometry for 3D surface profile. Recently, I’m working on image inpainting and super-resolution for tomographic reconstructions with sparse-view Computed Tomography (CT) and Magenetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) data.

I’m also broadly interested in learning computer theory and algorithms analysis in my spare time, such as probabilistic analysis of randomized algorithms.
